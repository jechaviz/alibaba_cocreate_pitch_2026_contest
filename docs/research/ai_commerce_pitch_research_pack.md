## Eligibility And Submission Signals

- Track: 0-to-1 Startup Track is explicitly for founders using Accio Work to bring raw ideas to life. This matches an AI-enabled sourcing/import startup concept better than the General SMEs Track.
- Applicant type: founders, SMEs, and students are all mentioned publicly. The Student Track has distinct eligibility and deadlines; use only if a current university student is the applicant.
- Product focus: official language repeatedly says product-based startup competition, product innovation, global sourcing, and product-driven businesses. A pure horizontal AI productivity tool would be weaker than a commerce/product launch tool.
- Accio Work dependency: application access and proposal preparation run through Accio Work/CoCreate Pitch Assistant. Evidence of meaningful Accio use is part of evaluation.
- Region: LA and London finals are confirmed with exact dates in the May 7 PR. Seoul/Korea is visible on the official pitch page and likely maps to the APAC regional expansion, but this needs live form confirmation.
- Prize expectations: public page says 50 percent cash and 50 percent Alibaba.com credits/coupons. Any financial forecast should treat prize winnings as mixed cash/platform value.
- Ticketing: pitch registration and CoCreate event tickets are separate; selected Pitch participants appear eligible for ticket fee refund.

## Opportunity Framing For Our Concept

Recommended concept frame: `AI Sourcing Copilot for Small Importers`

- Problem: small importers and first-time founders struggle to translate product ideas into supplier-ready specs, RFQs, landed-cost models, compliance checks, and launch plans.
- Solution: an Accio Work-powered agent workflow that converts a product idea into a supplier shortlist, RFQ package, negotiation plan, compliance/risk checklist, cost model, and storefront-ready launch assets.
- Why this fits Alibaba: it sits directly on Alibaba.com's global B2B commerce, supplier discovery, procurement, logistics, and AI agent story.
- Why this fits Accio Work: it demonstrates multi-agent execution, browser/web research, skills/plugins, approval gates, local-first auditability, and reusable workflows.
- What to show: one concrete product category, three supplier comparisons, MOQ/price/lead-time tradeoffs, landed cost, compliance warnings, launch copy, and a final recommendation.
- 90-second pitch spine: "Small importers lose weeks and make expensive mistakes before their first purchase order. We turn a product idea into a validated sourcing plan in one Accio Work session."

## Recommended Evidence Package

- One-page pitch summary with product name, customer, problem, solution, market, business model, and why Accio Work is central.
- Demo video or screen recording showing the workflow from idea to supplier-ready plan.
- Sanitized Accio Work transcript/export showing agents/skills used, prompts, outputs, and human approval points.
- Supplier shortlist table with source links, MOQ, unit price, lead time, certifications, trade terms, and risk notes.
- Landed-cost model with assumptions for duties, freight, payment terms, returns, and minimum viable order.
- Compliance checklist for the chosen product category and target market.
- Pitch deck and 90-second live pitch script.
- Founder/team profile and availability for LA/London final dates.
