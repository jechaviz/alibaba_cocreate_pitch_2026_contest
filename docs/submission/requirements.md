# Submission Requirements Map

Last updated: 2026-05-29  
Target: Alibaba CoCreate Pitch 2026 through Accio Work  
Working assumption: submit under 0-to-1 Startup Track unless live form evidence favors General SMEs Track.

## Confirmed Public Constraints

- Application path: install/download Accio Work, open Capabilities > Plugins, install "CoCreate Pitch 2026 Assistant", and complete the form with the Pitch Agent.
- Application email and Accio Work account email must match to claim the 1,000 bonus credits.
- Public evaluation signals: innovation, feasibility, market potential, Accio Work usage, product readiness, and demo quality.
- Regions publicly visible: Los Angeles, London, Seoul/Korea, and one coming-soon location. LA final is September 9-10, 2026; London final is November 19-20, 2026.
- Public prize signal: Gold USD 200K, Silver USD 100K, Bronze USD 50K, other finalists USD 10K. Official pitch page says awards are 50 percent cash and 50 percent Alibaba.com credits/coupons.
- Applicants may apply to multiple events, but participation appears limited to one event per applicant.

## Minimum Package For First Live Form Pass

1. Stable application email and Accio Work install with CoCreate Pitch 2026 Assistant.
2. Track and region choice: 0-to-1 Startup Track, LA primary unless strategic reason changes.
3. Product one-liner: "AI Sourcing Copilot for Small Importers turns a product idea into a supplier-ready RFQ, landed-cost model, compliance checklist, and launch plan in one Accio Work session."
4. Short summary, long pitch narrative, and 90-second pitch script.
5. Demo video draft and prototype link.
6. Accio Work evidence pack generated from a sanitized run.
7. Supplier/cost/compliance artifacts for one concrete product category.
8. Founder profile, team roles, availability, and consent/terms review notes.

## Live Form Validation Gaps

- Non-student application deadline.
- Character limits for text responses.
- Required versus optional uploads.
- Accepted file formats and maximum file sizes.
- Demo video length, hosting, and privacy settings.
- Whether prototype must be public.
- Whether deck is mandatory.
- Whether one applicant can submit multiple projects.
- Whether teams are allowed in 0-to-1 Startup Track.
- Whether LA/London/Korea forms differ.
- Whether product categories are restricted or sensitive categories are excluded.
- Full Terms and Conditions, privacy consent, publicity release, tax forms, and prize/coupon restrictions.
- Whether Accio Work-generated content must be disclosed or can be attached as evidence.

## Submission Hygiene

- Do not include passwords, API keys, private supplier quotes, private customer data, or confidential financial accounts in Accio Work artifacts.
- Use sanitized screenshots and tables. Blur account names, emails, local file paths, and live order data.
- Keep source links for public facts and supplier references in a separate appendix or hidden notes field only if allowed.
- Record final submission timestamp, selected region, selected track, confirmation email subject, and preview/edit link location without storing credentials.
