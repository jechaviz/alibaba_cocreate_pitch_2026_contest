Scenario: import a compact smart kitchen scale for direct-to-consumer and marketplace resale.

Currency: USD unless noted.

## Risk Adjustments

The demo risk score reduces confidence when:

- MOQ exceeds launch demand by more than 4 months.
- Lead time plus inspection time exceeds cash runway.
- Supplier response time is slower than 24 hours.
- Defect reserve is missing or below category benchmark.
- Compliance documents are absent for regulated categories.

## Submission Summary

The product makes money by reducing bad inventory buys. For the sample SKU, one avoided 500-unit mistake saves about `5,960` in inventory cash before ad spend. At `149/month`, the seller breaks even on the software if the copilot prevents one weak buy per year or improves one buy by roughly `12.50` per unit across a 150-unit launch.
