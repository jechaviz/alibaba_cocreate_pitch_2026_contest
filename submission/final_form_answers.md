## One-Line Pitch

Accio Commerce Copilot is the Accio Work decision cockpit for small importers
deciding their first purchase order.

## What Are You Building?

An AI commerce workflow that turns supplier options into a cash-risked buy,
negotiate, or pass plan. It combines supplier evidence, landed unit cost, MOQ
exposure, lead time, compliance risk, and storefront launch tasks into one
auditable cockpit.

## Problem

Small sellers can find suppliers, but they still struggle to know which quote
is worth buying. A cheap unit price can become a bad launch after freight,
duties, MOQ, defect reserve, lead time, and cash payback are considered.

## Solution

The MVP imports candidate suppliers, normalizes commercial terms, calculates
unit economics, scores risk, and generates a recommended buy plan with RFQ,
negotiation, launch, and reorder actions.

## AI Use

AI reconciles messy supplier inputs, explains risk drivers, drafts supplier
messages, produces storefront copy, and converts financial assumptions into
explainable recommendations while keeping assumptions visible for review.

## Target Customer

US and LATAM small importers, Amazon/Walmart/Shopify sellers, independent
retailers, and sourcing agencies that evaluate physical products without a
dedicated procurement finance team.

## Business Model

Starter SaaS at 149 USD/month, team plans for agencies, and usage-based exports
for supplier audit and launch evidence packets.

## Differentiation

Search tools find options, research tools estimate demand, freight tools quote
shipping, and spreadsheets require manual cleanup. Accio Commerce Copilot owns
the decision layer: buy, negotiate, or pass with evidence, economics, risk, and
next action in one workflow.

## Why Now

AI sourcing makes discovery faster, so the bottleneck shifts to decision
quality. Operators now need a fast, auditable way to decide whether a supplier
quote deserves the first purchase order.
