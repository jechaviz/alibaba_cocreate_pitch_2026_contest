# Judge Fast-Read

## Thesis

The winning insight is that supplier discovery is no longer the hard part.
The expensive failure is choosing the wrong first purchase order.

## Product

Accio Commerce Copilot is an AI decision cockpit for small importers. It turns
supplier candidates into a buy, negotiate, or pass plan using landed cost,
MOQ exposure, cash payback, supplier reliability, compliance risk, and launch
readiness.

## Why Alibaba / Accio

Accio Work helps entrepreneurs move from idea to sourced product. This product
extends that path into the operator decision: which supplier deserves money,
what to negotiate, and what evidence must exist before launch.

## Demo Path

1. Open the public demo.
2. Compare supplier quotes.
3. Watch the cheapest option lose when cash and risk are counted.
4. Review the recommended supplier and buy plan.
5. Open competition-aware judge mode.
