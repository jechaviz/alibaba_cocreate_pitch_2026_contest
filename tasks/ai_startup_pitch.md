# ai_startup_pitch

Objective: deliver a production-ready Alibaba CoCreate Pitch 2026 / Accio Work submission package for an AI commerce product.

Product working title: `Accio Commerce Copilot`

## Delivery Goals

1. Product pitch for an AI commerce startup.
2. Demo MVP that a judge can open and understand in under two minutes.
3. Unit economics with landed cost, margin, CAC, payback, supplier risk, and reorder logic.
4. Checklist for submission readiness and live-form validation.
5. Video outline with shot list and voiceover.
6. Submission packet with field-ready copy, links, evidence, and automation instructions.
7. Authorized automation scaffold for Accio Work / CoCreate Pitch form preparation.

## Product Thesis

Small importers can find products, but they struggle to prove whether a sourced SKU is worth buying before capital is locked in. The product turns supplier discovery into a decision cockpit: demand signal, supplier reliability, landed cost, compliance risk, cash conversion, and launch tasks in one AI-guided workflow.

## Judge Demo Path

1. Choose a target category.
2. Compare three supplier options.
3. Watch the economics panel update landed cost, gross margin, and payback.
4. Review risk explanations and mitigation tasks.
5. Export a submission/evidence packet.
